Executive Manager

Software Engineering Centre of Excellence
Hong Kong Jockey Club

About the Role

The Executive Manager, Software Engineering Centre of Excellence will be responsible for the management of the agile delivery process & quality of the Club's Middleware Platforms, CI/CD Pipelines and Test Automation teams.

Reporting to: Chief Technology Officer

Location: Hong Kong / Shenzhen Technology Centre

The Executive Manager will provide technical leadership for the continued maintenance and modernisation of the Middleware and Automation landscape of our Engineering teams. This role is responsible for code quality management & automation testing, development governance, and continuous delivery platform and processes, and their consistent use across all development teams.

The Executive Manager engages as a technical expert point between other engineering teams ensuring smooth project and programme execution in support of the IT change function against timelines and budget.

Key Responsibilities

Development Governance

  • Defines, deploys, maintains and ensures common coding standards and good coding practices
  • Implements the department's standard SDLC and best practices
  • Leads the adoption of DevSecOps and SRE within own department
  • Supports automated validation of code quality and sets appropriate KPIs

Quality & Test Management

  • Leads the Automation testing Strategy across functions
  • Ensures adequate human and testing resources are deployed effectively
  • Manages all aspects of automation testing including planning, execution and defect management

Agile Delivery

  • Owns the agile delivery process within the department
  • Ensures consistent and standardised implementation of Agile/Scrum
  • Continuously improves alignment with world class agile delivery methodologies
  • Achieves highly predictable, high quality, and efficient deliveries

Continuous Delivery

  • Leads adoption and continuous improvement of delivery methods and service level standards
  • Manages development process, highly automated delivery pipeline, and pre-production environments
  • Designs and applies high levels of automation across development teams
  • Implements change and release management strategy supporting CI/CT/CD

Engineering Excellence

  • Oversees the Engineering Guild to foster collaborative and innovative community
  • Develops and oversees comprehensive roadmap to implement continuous improvement initiatives
  • Fosters innovation-driven culture and promotes continuous improvement
  • Optimises engineering tooling & automation to enhance productivity

Qualifications & Experience

Education

University Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or equivalent. MBA degree is a plus.

Experience

15+ years of expertise in technology development with at least 8 years of management experience in a highly automated and scaled Agile IT development environment.

Leadership

8 years in people management and senior technical leadership positions. Proven track record in building and leading high performing technical delivery teams.

Domain Knowledge

10 years of working experience in backend transaction and information systems in either Horse Racing pari-mutuel systems, Totes, and/or Sportsbook fixed odds systems (i-gaming).

Management Experience Required

  • Scale at least 5 concurrent agile teams
  • DevOps/CICD/Env teams management
  • QA/Devtest/Automation teams leadership
  • Programme management experience in complex, large scale systems

Technical Skills

Agile at Scale DevOps CI/CT/CD SRE DevSecOps Microservices Event-Driven Architecture Message-Oriented Middleware High Transaction Systems Low Latency Systems Enterprise Architecture Cloud Hosted Applications
  • Strong background in technical software engineering and modern delivery techniques
  • Minimum 10 years of working experience on high transaction low latency systems
  • Deep familiarity of event driven micro service architectures using message-oriented middleware
  • Broad technical knowledge covering the Application Development life cycle
  • Knowledge of service management principles including SLAs and incident management
  • Experience with Enterprise Architecture frameworks is a strong plus

Key Success Factors
